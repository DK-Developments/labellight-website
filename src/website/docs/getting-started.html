Getting Started with Label Light

This guide will walk you through installing the Label Light extension, connecting your DYMO printer, and printing your first label from Lightspeed Retail.

Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have the following:

  • Google Chrome browser - Label Light is a Chrome extension and requires Chrome version 88 or later.
  • DYMO LabelWriter printer - Supported models include LabelWriter 450, 450 Turbo, 4XL, 550, and wireless models.
  • Lightspeed Retail (X-Series) account - You need an active Lightspeed Retail account to use this extension.
  • Active Label Light subscription - Subscribe here if you haven't already.

Installing DYMO Connect

Label Light communicates with your DYMO printer through DYMO Connect software. This software must be installed and running on your computer.

1

Download DYMO Connect

Visit the DYMO Support website and download DYMO Connect for your operating system (Windows or macOS).

‚ö†Ô∏è

Important

Download DYMO Connect (not DYMO Label Software). DYMO Connect includes the web service that Label Light requires to communicate with your printer.

2

Install DYMO Connect

Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions. The default installation options work for most users.

3

Connect Your Printer

Connect your DYMO LabelWriter to your computer via USB. DYMO Connect should automatically detect your printer.

4

Verify Web Service is Running

DYMO Connect runs a web service in the background that Label Light uses to print. Look for the DYMO icon in your system tray (Windows) or menu bar (macOS) to confirm it's running.

Tip

Enable "Start DYMO Connect at login" in the DYMO Connect preferences to ensure the web service is always available.
